Purpose

Resource Management provides analytical, developmental, budgetary, and communication support services for GTA. Additional services include providing audit case processing, internal training, and taxpayer education. The five sub-processes are: Compliance Standards, Financial Management, Planning and Performance Measures, Program Development and Support, and Taxpayer Education and Communication.

Goals

Provide timely and effective support to all processes within the program.
