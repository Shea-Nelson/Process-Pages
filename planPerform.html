Purpose

Program Evaluation - Provides analytical services to the GTA processes to help ensure effective and efficient utilization of program resources. Analytical Services - Consist of developing data to describe processes; evaluating programs; recommending feasible courses of action; acting as research and statistical consultants; and assisting in developing procedures and systems which support data utilization. Program Evaluation and Resource Allocation Analysis and Reporting - Assist in conducting return on investment (ROI) analyses for proposed and on-going programs. Provide the expertise to support GTA processes in the development, maintenance, and analysis of resource models which entail a monthly report cycle.

Activities

The Case Processing Sub-process is responsible for issuing to the taxpayer the Notice of Proposed Assessment for Compliance Determination Audit and Campaign files, processing all "special procedures" cases, including stipulation payment plans, bankruptcies and other cases which require coordination with the Compliance Enforcement Process. In addition to the above duties, a primary function of Case Processing is to handle taxpayer and auditor inquiries regarding the status of audit cases or other audit process problems after the issuance of the Notice of Proposed Assessment, including problems associated with the referral of uncollected audits to the downstream collection process.
