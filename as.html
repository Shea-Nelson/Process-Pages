Purpose

Account Services records tax revenues received, and timely and accurately reconciles and distributes refunds and distribution checks.

Activities

The account services process begins with the reconciliation and posting of daily revenue receipts to FLAIR and SUNTAX and preparation of the daily Collection Report. This report is a snap shot in time of State revenues collected by the Department listed by taxes, fees, and other agencies. Account Services also posts audit-related adjustments, warrant liabilities, and refunds to taxpayer accounts. The process ends when all validated receipts are posted to the appropriate fund and when all appropriate adjustments are accurately reflected on taxpayer accounts.
