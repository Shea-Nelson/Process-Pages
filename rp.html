Purpose

This process consists of Revenue Processing, Document Processing, and Data Capture. The purpose of this business process is to timely and accurately deposit and account for revenues, to receive electronic representations of taxrelated documents and to convert paper documents to electronic media.

Goals

Increase the percent of returns and remittances processed timely and accurately
