Purpose

Solution Support Services provides timely operational solutions and technical support to GTA business processes while minimizing user downtime that results in loss of time and money.

Activities

Solution Support Services provides support for GTA computer hardware and software, printers, all mobile devices, and networking. SSS is responsible for inventory as well as security of all computer equipment and storage devices. Solution Support staff work closely with ISP on computer security and encryption issues.
