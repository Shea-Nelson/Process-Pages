Purpose

Lead Development supplies leads to its key customers in the Compliance Determination process: Campaigns, Audit and Criminal Investigation. Lead development also serves those stakeholders who submit reward cases and tax violations to the General Tax Administration program.

  

Activities

Lead development uses data from a variety of sources to create leads. These sources include SUNTAX, internal referrals, tax violations and third party providers. Once created, leads are distributed to appropriate personnel located in over 30 field offices across Florida and our out-of-state offices. The process ends when the lead is distributed. Rewards applications (form DR-55) are thoroughly reviewed to determine validity and eligibility for compensation, and the claim is processed accordingly. Tax violation applications are reviewed for validity and often result in an educational opportunity for the taxpayer rather than a lead or case. The rewards and violations process ends when the case is closed.
