Purpose

The Business Technology Office provides support for the various GTA processes.  The three support services areas are: GTA System Support (GSS), Data Support Services (DSS) and Solution Support Services (SSS).  These service areas support all GTA technology, integrated tax system, local hardware, and software needs.

Goal

Provide support services for the GTA process areas including management of technology projects, systems analysis and design for system enhancements and coordination of user acceptance testing, to support of user technology issues, and technology inventory tracking.
