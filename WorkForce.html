Purpose

SUNTAX Support provides operational support to GTA employees in the areas of technology, communications, and tax law.

Activities

SUNTAX Support performs a variety of activities to provide GTA personnel support needed to help perform their jobs. These include:

  • Intaking and triaging systems issues, including SUNTAX, IMS, remote capture, and credit card transactions. This process begins with submission of a STAIRS request or phone call by system users. SUNTAX Support evaluates the reported issue, researches possible causes and resolution, and resolves the issue or routes it to the appropriate area for resolution.
  • Developing and distributing operational communications such as system alerts, announcements of system enhancements or tax law changes, and weekly and monthly newsletters.
  • Partnering with Program Training to develop and deliver certain tax law training to employees.
  • Maintaining required SUNTAX training within the Learning Management System. This training ensures all users complete a minimum level of training prior to gaining SUNTAX access and provides a foundation for on-the-job training conducted by the business processes.
