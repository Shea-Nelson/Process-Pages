Purpose

Vouching reconciles and distributes approved refund checks, and assists with the timely and accurate distribution of funds to local governments and State agencies.

Activities

The vouching process begins with the approval of a refund and the transfer of revenues to the appropriate State agency or local government. The vouching unit reconciles approved refunds for payment to the Comptroller’s Office daily payment register to ensure accuracy and that payment is sent the appropriate recipient. The vouching unit is also responsible for transmitting documentation to the Comptroller’s Office to ensure the transfer of funds to the appropriate State agency and local government. The process ends when the taxpayer receives the refund payment or the appropriate State agency or local government receives the revenue payment.
