Purpose

Provide comprehensive operational support and training to ensure program wide consistency and continuity.

Activities

Establish and communicate compliance standards that promote fair and effective enforcement of Florida tax laws,
Oversee the development and delivery of training courses and materials for GTA,
Monitor compliance cases to ensure adherence to established standards, and
Administer the Department's Certified Audit and Voluntary Disclosure programs.
