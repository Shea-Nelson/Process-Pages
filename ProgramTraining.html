Purpose

Program Training researches, designs, develops, and delivers training curriculum and training courses in response to legislative mandates, system changes, and program needs.

Activities

Program Training provides support to the GTA program by:
  • Analyzing new legislation and business process changes to determine the impact on courses and course materials.
  • Providing technical assistance to GTA processes.
  • Coordinating continuing education opportunities.
  • Conducting problem, job task, process, and training needs analyses to determine training and educational needs.
